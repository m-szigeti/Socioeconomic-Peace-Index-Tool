About the Socioeconomic Peace Index Tool

The Socioeconomic Peace Index (SEPI) online tool helps researchers, policymakers, and practitioners explore and interpret socioeconomic and peace-related indicators on an interactive map. By combining curated data with clear visualization, it supports evidence-based discussion and planning in fragile and development contexts.

Our mission

We aim to make complex socioeconomic and peace data easier to access and understand, so stakeholders can see patterns, compare areas, and connect analysis to action—especially where institutions and communities are under stress.
