Socio Economic Peace Index Tool

🕊️ Socioeconomic Peace Index

What is SEPI?

The Socioeconomic Peace Index measures regional peace by combining five key development factors.

The Five Pillars:

📚 Education: School enrollment and literacy rates

🌾 Food Security: Household food access and nutrition

💰 Poverty Reduction: Income levels and economic opportunities

🏥 Health Access: Healthcare facilities and services

🌍 Climate Resilience: Environmental vulnerability factors

Score Ranges:

0.8-1.0: Very High Peace | 0.6-0.8: High Peace | 0.4-0.6: Moderate | 0.2-0.4: Low | 0.0-0.2: Very Low
